Creative Works

This section includes my creative works such as drawings and photographs created over the years.

Drawings

I began drawing at the age of eight, often relying on simple tools and limited materials. Through years of independent practice, I explored anatomy, shading, and character design as ways to express ideas visually. Beyond technique, drawing has served as a source of comfort and reflection, helping me strengthen both creativity and resilience.

Photos

I first became interested in photography by capturing images of beautiful scenery around me. As I grew more comfortable with the camera, I began taking on the role of photographer for my club, projects, and class events. It has become a way for me to hold onto memories while contributing to the collective story of the communities I'm part of.
